About Us

Founded in 2018, CynovaIT has established itself as a trusted Managed Service Provider (MSP), delivering tailored IT solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of modern businesses. We specialize in Network and Infrastructure Management, Data & Electrical Cabling, and IT Workforce as a Service (WFaaS), ensuring reliable and scalable support for our clients.

  • Our Journey

    Established in 2018, CynovaIT delivers innovative and scalable IT solutions.

  • Our Commitment

    We provide cutting-edge technology and expert support for seamless operations.

  • Our Vision

    CynovaIT empowers businesses with tailored IT services to drive efficiency and growth.

At CynovaIT, we understand the challenges of today’s evolving digital landscape. With services like IT Deployment & Installation, IT Project Management, and IT Relocations, we empower businesses with seamless operations, enhanced connectivity, and efficient transitions, all while ensuring cost-effectiveness and flexibility. Driven by innovation and a client-first approach, we deliver cutting-edge solutions that let you focus on what matters most—growing your business.
